About Me

Hi, I'm Marco Astorga! I'm a father of two kids and originally from Chile . But, for the past one year and six months, I've been living in Kitchener, Canada . My native language is Spanish, and in my free time, I love to play Minecraft and learn about cars. And, I've even started my own YouTube channel where I share my life with others.

Chile is a country located in South America and is known for its long and narrow shape. It's got a diverse landscape, with everything from the Andes mountains to deserts, lakes, and the Pacific Ocean. Chilean culture is a mix of indigenous, European, and African influences and it shows in its traditional music, dance, and food. Chilean cuisine is diverse, with dishes like asado (barbecue), pastel de choclo (a corn and beef casserole), and the famous empanada, which is a turnover filled with various ingredients.

Join me on my YouTube channel where I play Minecraft and bring to life exciting stories through my gameplay. I love to get creative and immerse myself in this virtual world, and I invite you to join me on this journey. Whether you're a fan of Minecraft or just looking for some fun entertainment, my channel has got you covered. So, hit the subscribe button now and let's play!
